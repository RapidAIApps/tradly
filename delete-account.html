Account Deletion Instructions

You can delete your Good Habits account and associated data in two ways:

Option 1: Delete through the app

  1. Open the Good Habits app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Request deletion via email

  1. Send an email to support@rapidaiapps.com
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Good Habits"
  3. Include your email address used for the account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

What gets deleted:

  • Your account information
  • All habit tracking data
  • App settings and preferences
  • Usage history
  • Subscription information

Need Help?

If you have any questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us at support@rapidaiapps.com
