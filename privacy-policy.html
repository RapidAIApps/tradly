Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2024

This privacy policy applies to the Tradly app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Rapid AI Apps (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

1. Personally Identifiable Information:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • User ID (automatically generated)
  • Device ID (for notifications)
  • Advertising ID (for AdMob)
  • App Instance ID

2. Usage Information:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • Habit tracking data and completion records
  • App usage patterns and session information
  • Time zone (for habit scheduling)
  • The time spent on the Application
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

3. Subscription Information:

  • Subscription status
  • Purchase history
  • Transaction IDs

The Application does not collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos/Media
  • Calendar information
  • Biometric data
  • Social media information

Third Party Access

The Application utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Authentication and Analytics)
  • Supabase (Data Storage)
  • RevenueCat (Subscription Management)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

Opt-Out Rights

You can:

  • Purchase ad removal
  • Upgrade to Premium for ad-free experience
  • Reset advertising ID through device settings
  • Uninstall the application to stop all data collection

Data Retention Policy

  • Active account data: Stored until account deletion
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Analytics data: Anonymized after 1 year

To delete your data, contact support@rapidaiapps.com. Premium and Pro users can export their data before deletion.

Children

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact support@rapidaiapps.com for immediate data deletion.

Security

We implement:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Protected database access

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to:

  • The collection and use of information as outlined in this policy
  • The processing of your information for the stated purposes
  • Receiving important notifications about your habits and account
